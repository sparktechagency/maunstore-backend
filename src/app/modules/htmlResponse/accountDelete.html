How to Delete Your Account - Raconli Group

  1. Tap the Menu (☰) icon on the top right corner to open the sidebar.
    The sidebar will slide in from the left side.
  2. From the sidebar, scroll down and tap Delete Account .
    You’ll see a confirmation pop-up next.
  3. When the pop-up appears, enter your Password and tap Delete to confirm.
    Your account will be permanently deleted, and you will be signed out automatically.
